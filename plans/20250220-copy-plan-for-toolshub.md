**Project**: Free online tools hub (50+ AI tools)
**Date**: 2026-02-20
**Status**: Planning

---

## Brand Voice & Tone Guidelines

### Core Personality
- **Direct**: No fluff, no marketing jargon
- **Helpful**: Focus on user's immediate problem
- **Transparent**: Honest about what tools do
- **Accessible**: Simple language, no technical assumptions

### Voice Characteristics
| Trait | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| Action-oriented | Verbs first, outcomes clear | "Remove background" not "Background removal solution" |
| Specific | Numbers over vague claims | "50+ tools" not "Many tools" |
| Casual but professional | Friendly without being informal | "Hey" = no. "Get started" = yes |
| Benefit-focused | What user gets, not what we have | "Summarize in seconds" not "Advanced AI algorithm" |

### Words to Use
- Free, instant, click, browser-based, private, secure
- Tools, utilities, converters, generators
- Students, professionals, creators, developers

### Words to Avoid
- "Revolutionary", "game-changing", "state-of-the-art"
- "Leverage", "synergy", "optimize", "paradigm"
- "Best", "#1", "leading" (unless verified)

## Writing Checklist

Before finalizing copy:
- [ ] Each section has ONE clear message
- [ ] Conversion action is obvious
- [ ] SEO keyword naturally included (1-2x)
- [ ] Benefit stated before feature
- [ ] Specificity over vague claims
- [ ] Tone consistent (helpful, direct)
- [ ] No marketing jargon
- [ ] Claims verifiable
- [ ] Mobile length considered (headlines < 60 chars)
